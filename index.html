KAIRI MEAD

The Spirit of an Island, Bottled.

Kairi Mead is more than a drink; it's a story whispered by the ancient forests of Trinidad. Once called 'Kairi' – Land of the Hummingbird – by the indigenous Amerindians, our island’s spirit is captured in every drop. Crafted using pure, local honey and time-honored methods unique to our shores, our mead is a tribute to the Northern Range. Here, bees dance among wildflowers, gathering nectar that becomes the golden heart of Newton-Forrest.
