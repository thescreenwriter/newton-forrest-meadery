OUR STORY

It all begins with the honey. For generations, our family has kept bees in the high slopes of Trinidad's Northern Range, a partnership with the land that yields a honey with the wild taste of a protected ecosystem. We believe the best mead is a conversation between this extraordinary ingredient and time, and our family's story is deeply tied to that simple tradition.

OUR RANGE

Every mead we create begins with the same extraordinary Northern Range honey, but from there, the story changes with each bottle. You can explore the pure, traditional taste of the mountains in our classic meads, or discover how that same honey pairs with fresh island fruits and Caribbean spices in our melomels and metheglins. Each offers a different way to experience the flavor of our home.

OUR COMMITMENT TO TRANSPARENCY

We believe you have the right to know exactly what's in your mead. Our promise of quality is not just a story. It is verifiable on the blockchain. We use this immutable ledger to secure a digital record of each bottle's journey: from the specific hives it came from, through our meadery, to you. It is a modern maker's mark, our guarantee of provenance.

The Honey

The soul of our mead. Our unprocessed honey comes from bees that feed on the diverse wildflowers of the Northern Range, creating complex flavors impossible to replicate.

The Fruits

For our melomels and metheglins, we source our fruits and spices from local farms, ensuring the best flavour and supporting our community.

The Packaging

Our bottles, crafted from 100% post-consumer recycled glass, embrace a wabi-sabi aesthetic. We use cork-based closures, natural beeswax seals, and compostable labels printed with algae-based inks.
