Northern Range Honey

The soul of our mead. Our unprocessed honey comes from bees that feed on the diverse wildflowers of our mountains, creating complex flavors impossible to replicate.

Local Fruits & Spices

For our melomels and metheglins, we source our fruits and spices from local farms, ensuring peak freshness and supporting our community.

Sustainable Packaging

Our bottles, crafted from 100% post-consumer recycled glass, embrace a wabi-sabi aesthetic. We use cork-based closures, natural beeswax seals, and compostable labels printed with algae-based inks.
