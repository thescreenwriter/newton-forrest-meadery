OUR STORY

It all begins with the honey. For generations, our family has kept bees in the high slopes of Trinidad's Northern Range, a partnership with the land that yields a honey with the wild taste of a protected ecosystem. We believe the best mead is a conversation between this extraordinary ingredient and time, and our family's story is deeply tied to that simple tradition.

OUR RANGE

Every mead we create begins with certified Northern Range wildflower honey, but from there, the story changes with each bottle. You can explore the pure, traditional taste of the mountains in our classic meads, or discover how that same honey pairs with fresh island fruits and Caribbean spices in our melomels and metheglins.

OUR COMMITMENT

Our promise of quality is not mere hype. It is verifiable on the blockchain. We use this immutable ledger to secure a digital record of each bottle's journey: from the specific hive, through our meadery, to you. It is a modern maker's mark, our guarantee of provenance.

The Honey

The soul of our mead is the honey. It comes from Trinidad’s Northern Range, one of the few places left in the world that can produce a harvest free of medications and synthetic antibiotics. Our bees forage on diverse wildflowers, creating a raw, complex flavor impossible to replicate.

The Fruits

It continues with our ingredients. For our melomels and metheglins, we source our fruits and spices from Regenerative Organic Certified® local farms, ensuring the best flavour and supporting our community.

The Packaging

It ends with the bottle. We use recycled glass, plant-based closures, and compostable labels printed with algae-based inks. A mead this honest deserves a bottle to match.
